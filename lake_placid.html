Site of the 1932 and 1980 Winter Olympics

Lake Placid is one of the Adirondacks' most significant historical cities. Not only was this city the site of the Winter Olympics two times, the city has participated in multiple recreational events. Some of the event include the Ironman Triathlon, ice dance competitions, and the annual Winter Carnival.
